Cookies

Description

Cranberries, walnuts and chocolate chips are in every single bite. These oatmeal cookies will for sure satisfy your sweet tooth and give you lots of goodness at the same time. Not only kids love them, but grown-ups also can not stop reaching for more.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. butter
  • 1 c. brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 1/2 c. rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 c. ap flour
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 c. chocolate chips
  • 1/2 c. cranberries
  • 1/2 c. walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375℉ (190℃) F (190 degrees C).
  2. Line the baking sheets with baking mats or parchment paper. Set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, add together butter, brown sugar, egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the oats, flour, baking soda and salt, and mix well.
  5. Add the dried cranberries, walnuts, and chocolate chips, gently stir until evenly distributed.
  6. Drop by rounded measuring tablespoonfuls (15 mL) of dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheet.
  7. Bake one baking sheet at a time for 11 to 13 minutes or until lightly browned.
  8. Cool 2 minutes on the baking sheet on a wire rack.
